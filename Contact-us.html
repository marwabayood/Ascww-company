More Info

The Assiut and New Valley Water and Wastewater Company was established in 1981 as a public sector company for the purpose of managing, operating and developing water facilities in the various governorate departments. With the issuance of Republican Decree No. 135 of 2004, the company became one of the subsidiaries of the Holding Company for Potable Water and Wastewater.
